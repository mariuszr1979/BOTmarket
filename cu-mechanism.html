CU = Measurement, Not Negotiation

A concrete, physical unit — like a kilowatt-hour or a byte.

Definition
1 CU = 1 millisecond of GPU compute
on the exchange reference hardware (NVIDIA A100 equivalent, updated quarterly)
📏

Unit is Fixed

1 CU always = 1ms of GPU time. The definition never changes. Like a meter is always a meter.

📊

Price Floats

Services price above or below actual compute cost. Market determines the price, not the unit.

🔌

kWh Analogy

A kWh is always a kWh. But the price of a kWh varies by provider, location, and demand. CU works the same way.
